# LZSA1 / LZSA2 license

LZAN ships the LZSA1/LZSA2 encoders (`src/lzsa1.rs`, `src/lzsa2.rs`) and the ported 6502 decoders (`decrunchers/lzsa1-marty*.s`, `decrunchers/lzsa2-marty*.s`, and the speed-optimized `decrunchers/lzsa1-brandwood-faster.s`). The upstream work is by Emmanuel Marty (Zlib) and John Brandwood (Boost Software License 1.0).

Two licenses apply. The LZSA1/LZSA2 format, the encoders, and the ported "small" 6502 decoders (`decompress_small_v1.asm`, `decompress_small_v2.asm`) are Zlib, Copyright (c) 2019 Emmanuel Marty. The speed-optimized forward 6502 decoder `decrunchers/lzsa1-brandwood-faster.s` is a port of John Brandwood's `decompress_faster_v1.asm` (`lzsa1_6502.s`), under the Boost Software License 1.0, Copyright John Brandwood 2021. Upstream `matchfinder.c` is CC0 but was not used here; the match finder is reimplemented. Both texts follow.

Upstream (Marty, Zlib): https://github.com/emmanuel-marty/lzsa/blob/master/LICENSE.zlib.md
Upstream (Brandwood faster decoder): https://github.com/emmanuel-marty/lzsa/blob/master/asm/6502/decompress_faster_v1.asm
